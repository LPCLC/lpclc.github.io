Business Regulations

The world runs on commerce, money, and free trade of goods and labor. At a basic level, labor income is the lifeline of a family's household. When the Government restricts the ability of a person to transact business or work in certain industries, it makes more difficult the life of the household. Except when absolutely needed, the Government should have no ability to restrict businesses with added regulations.

Liberty County's Council has repeatedly voted to require more bureaucracy paperwork for small businesses during nationwide economic turmoil. Joesph Smith will never vote to increase business taxes or other regulations in Liberty County.

Localized Fiscal Responsibility

Last year, the Liberty County Council spent thousands of dollars on attorneys fees to investigate an ethics complaint against one of their own Aldermen. Weeks later, the attorney resigned the case, citing the irresponsible and personally repulsive behavior of the council. Following this resignation, another attorney was retained who had to begin their work from square one. This feat led to a complete and utter waste of taxpayer dollars because Liberty County's Council couldn't maintain professionalism for a serious matter.

Every dollar brought into Liberty County's control was done by theft: all taxation is theft. If they're going to wasting the money, they don't deserve it. At the very least, every dollar should produce a dollar's worth of value. Joseph Smith will stretch every dollar possible so as to avoid increasing taxes.
